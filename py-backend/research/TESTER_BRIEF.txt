ROLL-CALL RESEARCH SESSION — TESTER BRIEF
==========================================

Thank you for helping with our attendance-system study. This takes about 3–4 minutes.

WHAT WE'RE TESTING
------------------
A face-scan attendance system (like phone Face ID) for classrooms. Your participation helps us measure accuracy and speed.

WHAT HAPPENS TO YOUR DATA
-------------------------
1. LIVE SCAN (during the session)
   - Your face photo is sent to our server for enrollment + verification.
   - We record how long the server takes to process each scan (milliseconds).
   - Data is stored in a secure MongoDB database for research only.

2. SAVED IMAGES (on the research laptop)
   - We save ~5 photos of you (straight on, left, right, closer, farther).
   - Used OFFLINE after the session to test recognition under different poses/lighting.
   - NOT posted publicly. Used only for accuracy statistics in our paper.

3. WHAT WE DO NOT DO
   - We do not sell your data.
   - We do not use your photos for training commercial AI.
   - We do not share identifiable images outside the research team.

You may request deletion of your data after the project.

WHAT YOU DO (3–4 MINUTES)
-------------------------
1. Sit in front of the laptop camera.
2. Follow on-screen icons (arrows for left/right, etc.).
3. When you see "1 face OK — press SPACE", press the SPACE bar.
4. Repeat for 5 quick poses. Done.

No account needed. You'll be assigned an ID like P02, P03, etc.

REQUIREMENTS
------------
- Remove glasses/hats if possible (or keep consistent across poses).
- Good lighting on your face.
- One person at a time.

QUESTIONS? Contact Japheth before starting.
